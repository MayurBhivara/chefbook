Chicken Minced Salad

A crunchy mixed chicken salad cooked with Thai herbs and served with a bowl of fresh vegetables and hot sticky rice.

  • Ingridents
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 green chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Â¼ cup crushed and roasted peanuts

Method

Sprinkle salt, paprika powder and drizzle lemon juice over the chicken.

Heat oil in a non-stick frill pan, place chicken and cook till the grill marks appear on both the sides. Cool.

Roughly chop chicken. Transfer into a bowl.

Add rice, tomatoes and mix well.

Add celery, chillies, mint leaves and mix well.

Add sugar, salt and pepper powder and mix well.

Serve immediately garnished with peanuts
