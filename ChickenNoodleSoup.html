Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken Noodle Soup. This is one recipe that we cook so often because it's the best and most delicious way to get our veggies in with no one complaining.

  • Ingridents
  • 3 tbs of butter
  • 2 chopped up ribs of celery
  • 2 xhopped up carrots
  • 1 chopped medium onion
  • 1 tbs of minced garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • chopped chicken
  • 2 bouillion cubes
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp whole oregano
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • noodles
  • parsley flakes

Method

Place pot over high heat.... add 3 tbs of butter, 2 chopped up ribs of celery, 2 chopped up carrots, 1 chopped medium onion, 1 tsp of minced garlic and 2 bay leaves.

After 15 minutes or once veggies are tender, add chopped chicken.

Once chicken is done cut the fire off

Season with 2 Chicken Bouillon Cubes, 1/2 tsp of Thyme seasoning, 1/2 tsp Whole Oregano, 1/2 tsp Black Pepper, and 2 tsp of Salt

Remove 2 Bay Leafs

Turn fire back on..... Add Chicken broth, water, and noodles and cook on low heat for 8 minutes until done Sprinkle soup with Parsley Flakes.
