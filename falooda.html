Falooda

The falooda is a dessert disguised as a drink. It is a felicitous coupling of silky vermicelli noodles with ice cream and /or crushed ice, slithery black basil seeds (tukmaria), all of which is steeped in a colourful bath of rose and khus syrup, with nuts sprinkled over.

  • STRAWBERRY JELLY
  • CHILLED ROSE MILK
  • COOKED SEV
  • SOAKED SABJA SEEDS
  • ROSE SYRUP
  • VANILLA ICE CREAM
  • CHOPPED NUTS (ALMOND & PISTACHIO)
  • TUTTI FRUTTI
  • READYMADE JELLY CRYSTAL PACKET
  • BOILED MILK - 1 1/2 LITRE
  • ROSE SYRUP - 2 TBSP
  • SUGAR - 1 1/2 TBSP

Method

Step 1 is to make the strawberry jelly. For this, follow the instructions behind the store bought jelly crystals and make the jelly. It takes about 45 mins to set in room temperature. You can prepare this earlier and keep in fridge as well.

Step 2 is to make the rose milk. For this boil and cool down milk. Add rose syrup, sugar according to your requirement. Mix well and keep it in fridge to chill.

Step 3 is to soak the sabja seeds. Take the sabja seeds in a bowl and add enough water to it. Let is sit for 5 mins or more.

Step 4 is to cook the sev. Boil some water and add the sev to it. Cook for 1 mins and strain it. Keep it aside.

Step 5 is to assemble the falooda. Take a tall glass, start with strawberry jelly. Add some cooked sev and drizzle some rose syrup over it. Now add the soaked sabja seeds. Pour the chilled rose milk till about half the glass. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and sprinkle some chopped nuts(badam,pista). Add chopped apple and banana. Another scoop of jelly, sabja seeds and ice cream Drizzle some rose syrup and sprinkle with nuts, tutti fruitt. Finally garnish it with a cherry on top.
