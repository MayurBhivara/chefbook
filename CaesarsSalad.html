Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad (also spelled Cesar and Cesare) is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice (or lime juice), olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

  • Ingridents
  • 3 slices white bread, cut into cubes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup vegetarian mayonnaise
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper as required
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 1/4 th cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3 tbsp chilled water
  • 2 bunches romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated

Method

Chop the bread into cubes ( size depends on how you prefer )

For dressing in a bowl add mayonnaise, Salt to taste, Pepper, parsley, garlic, Parmesan cheese and chilled water

Assemble them by adding the lettuce leaves which are chopped then add croutons the dressing and Parmesan cheese Enjoy the yummy & delicious Caesar Salad!
