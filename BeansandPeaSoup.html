Beans and Pea Soup

Creamed green peas soup is also fantastic to make ahead of time for the week ahead when you need a quick yet nourishing lunch, dinner, or side to a meal. We are going to make cream based soup flavored with mint and coriander.

  • Ingridents
  • Green peas 200gm
  • Onion 1 no
  • Garlic cloves 5
  • Fresh mint 10 leaves
  • Coriander 5-6 sprigs
  • Cooking cream 3 tbsp
  • Butter 2 tsp
  • Room temperature water 200 ml
  • Olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Sugar 1tsp
  • Salt and black pepper as per taste.

Method

Heat the pan and drizzle olive oil.

Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until golden brown.

Sprinkle salt and black pepper and add green peas, some fresh chopped mint, coriander and sugar.

Cook for a while and add room temperature water and let it simmer for 5-10 min.

Remove the pan from heat and let it cool for a while and put the mixture into blender.

Blend until a smooth paste and strain it through a strainer.

Take the strained mixture in a pan and add some more water.

Give a gentle boil and finish with cream and butter.

Add some chopped mint and coriander and garnish by drizzling cream and olive oil.

Serve hot.
